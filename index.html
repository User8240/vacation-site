Reason:

If I could travel anywhere, I'd absolutely want to go to Korea! I'm a big fan of Korean food, and have always wanted a spa-getaway vacation. Korea would be the most optimal place to do that!

Explanation:

They've got some of the best skin care, amongst other things relating to cosmetics. Not to mention, it's very common there to go get facials and massages and such so it's much more affordable!

Conclusion:

If I could go there and take care of my skin and body, while also eating some of my favorite foods, that would hands down be the best vacation ever!
